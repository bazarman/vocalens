Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 2026

VocaLens (“we”, “our”, or “us”) respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how information is handled when you use the VocaLens mobile application and website.

How VocaLens Works

VocaLens is a text-photo translator. The app allows users to take a photo, extract text from the image, and translate the text into another language.

Camera Usage

VocaLens uses the device camera to capture images for text recognition. Camera access is used solely for this core functionality.

Image Processing

All image processing and text recognition occur locally on the user’s device. Images are not uploaded, stored, or transmitted to external servers.

Text Processing and Translation

After text is extracted from an image, the text content may be sent to a language model (LLM) service for translation. Only the extracted text is processed for this purpose.

The translated text is returned to the device and is not stored by VocaLens. No images or personally identifiable information are included in this process.

Data Collection and Storage

VocaLens does not collect, store, or share personal data. No personally identifiable information (PII) is collected or retained.

Advertising

VocaLens displays advertisements provided by third-party partners, including AppLovin. These partners may collect limited, non-personal data such as device information or ad interaction data, in accordance with their own privacy policies.

Third-Party Services

VocaLens does not control how third-party services handle data. Users are encouraged to review the privacy policies of these services for more information.

Children’s Privacy

VocaLens does not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Changes to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page.

Contact

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us once official contact details are available.
