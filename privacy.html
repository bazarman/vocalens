Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 2026

VocaLens (“we”, “our”, or “us”) respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how information is handled when you use the VocaLens mobile application and website.

Camera Permission

VocaLens uses the device camera to recognize and translate text. Camera access is required solely for this core functionality.

Data Collection and Storage

VocaLens does not collect, store, or share personal data. No personally identifiable information (PII) is collected.

Image Processing

All images captured by the app are processed only on the user’s device. Images are not transmitted to external servers, stored, or retained after processing.

Advertising

VocaLens displays advertisements provided by third-party partners, including AppLovin. These advertising partners may collect limited, non-personal data (such as device information or ad interaction data) in accordance with their own privacy policies.

Third-Party Services

VocaLens does not control how third-party advertising partners handle data. Users are encouraged to review the privacy policies of these services for more information.

Children’s Privacy

VocaLens does not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe that personal data has been provided by a child, please contact us so we can take appropriate action.

Changes to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page.

Contact

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us once official contact details are available.
