Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 2026

VocaLens respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how information is handled when you use the VocaLens application and website.

Camera Usage

VocaLens uses the device camera to recognize and translate text. Camera access is used solely for this purpose.

Data Collection

VocaLens does not store, collect, or share personal data. No personally identifiable information is collected.

Image Processing

All images captured by the app are processed only on the device. Images are not uploaded to external servers or stored after processing.

Advertising

Advertising is provided by third-party partners, including AppLovin. These partners may collect non-personal, anonymized data as governed by their own privacy policies.

Third-Party Privacy Policies

VocaLens is not responsible for the privacy practices of third-party advertising partners. We encourage users to review the privacy policies of these services.

Changes to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page.

Contact

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us once official contact details are available.
