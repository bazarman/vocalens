Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 2026

VocaLens (“we”, “our”, or “us”) respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how information is handled when you use the VocaLens mobile application and website.

How VocaLens Works

VocaLens is a text photo translator. The app allows users to take a photo, extract text from the image, and translate that text into another language.

Camera Usage

VocaLens uses the device camera to capture images for text recognition. Camera access is required solely for this functionality.

Image and Text Recognition (OCR)

Text recognition is performed using Google ML Kit. Image processing and text extraction occur on the user’s device. Images are not uploaded, stored, or transmitted to external servers.

Text Translation

After text is extracted from an image, the text may be sent to ChatGPT-5 mini, a language model service, for translation. Only the extracted text is transmitted for this purpose.

No images, camera data, or personally identifiable information are sent. Translated text is returned to the device.

Local Storage

Users may choose to save images, extracted text, or translated text on their own device. This data is stored locally under the user’s control and is not accessed, collected, transmitted, or backed up by VocaLens.

User-Initiated Sharing

VocaLens allows users to share images and/or translated text using the device’s built-in sharing features. Sharing is entirely optional and initiated by the user.

VocaLens does not receive, store, or process any content that users choose to share. Shared content is handled according to the privacy policies of the destination apps or services selected by the user.

Data Collection and Storage

VocaLens does not collect, store, or share personal data. No personally identifiable information (PII) is collected.

Advertising

VocaLens displays advertisements provided by third-party partners, including AppLovin. These partners may collect limited, non-personal data such as device information or ad interaction data, in accordance with their own privacy policies.

Third-Party Services

VocaLens integrates third-party services for text recognition, translation, and advertising. VocaLens does not control how these services handle data. Users are encouraged to review their respective privacy policies.

Children’s Privacy

VocaLens does not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Changes to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page.

Contact

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us once official contact details are available.
